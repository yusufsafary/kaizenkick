FAQ

Questions

What is KaizenKick? +
KaizenKick is a football improvement platform where you can create custom player cards, track your progress, and earn rewards through the $KAIZENKICK token on Solana.
How do I get $KAIZENKICK tokens? +
Visit orytnh.dev to trade and get more information about $KAIZENKICK on the Solana network. Follow @kainzenkick on X for the latest updates.
Is the card generator free? +
Yes! The basic card generator is completely free. No account needed. Token holders will get access to exclusive card frames and perks in future updates.
Which blockchain is $KAIZENKICK on? +
$KAIZENKICK is live on Solana — chosen for its high speed, low transaction fees, and large, active community.
What does "Kaizen" mean? +
改善 (Kaizen) is a Japanese word meaning "continuous improvement." It's our philosophy: small improvements every day lead to extraordinary results — in football and in life.
